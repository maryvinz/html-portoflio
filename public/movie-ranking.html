The Best Movies According to Mary

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Minion Movie

Silly and cute. Gave lots of laughs

The Bee Movie

A classic. Child friendly with adult humor and gets funnier as you get older. Good lessons to learn.

Puss in Boots

So good I watched it twice. Lots of adventure. There's a lot of valuable lessons to learn throughout the movie and almost made me cry at one point
